Privacy Policy

LockSure · Effective date: 29 June 2026

LockSure ("the App") is a departure checklist app developed by Marco Troian ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains how we handle your information.

What Data We Collect

LockSure is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect personal information.

  • Checklist data — Your checklists, items, and settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No analytics or tracking — We do not collect usage analytics, behavioural data, or any personally identifiable information (PII). Nothing about how you use the App is sent to us or any third party.
  • Purchase data — If you subscribe, RevenueCat processes your payment. We do not see or store your payment details.

How We Use It

  • Your checklist data is used solely to provide the App's functionality on your device.
  • We have no servers that receive your personal data.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat — Manages subscription purchases only (used solely if you choose to subscribe). Their processing is subject to the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

We do not use advertising services, analytics services, or sell any data to third parties.

Permissions

  • Location (optional) — Used for geofence-based departure reminders. Location data is processed on-device only and is never transmitted to any server.
  • Notifications (optional) — Used to send you reminder alerts. Notification content is generated locally.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not retain any personal data on servers. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the App.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. Your data lives entirely on your device under your control.

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know that we do not sell personal information. If you are in the EEA/UK, we confirm we do not process personal data beyond what is described above.

For any privacy-related requests, contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

LockSure is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
